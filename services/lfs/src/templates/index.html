Jelajahi Fitur

Toolkit Intuitif Kami

Tiga pilar utama untuk membantu Anda mengungkap kebenaran digital dengan cepat dan akurat.

Disk Image Triage

Performs a quick filesystem triage to list contents of a raw disk image. Useful for initial assessment of removable media.

PDF Content Analysis

Extracts textual content and analyzes embedded document actions for potential scripts. Ideal for examining suspicious documents.

PNG Metadata Inspector

Displays core image metadata and renders custom text-based annotations found within the image data structure.
